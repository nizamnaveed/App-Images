Q: What is a Halan account?
A: Halan account is a mobile app that lets you perform a variety of financial transactions such as cash deposits, fund transfers, bill payments, voucher payments, school fee payments, government payments, and more anytime, anywhere! You can open it simply by downloading the Halan app on your phone, and it will be linked to your mobile number on any mobile network.

Q: Who can open a Halan account?
A: Anyone aged 18 or above who has a valid CNIC and a mobile number registered in
their name can open a Halan account.

Q: Why do I need to provide my CNIC number and issue date when opening an account?
A: Your Halan account is authenticated via NADRA, and for this process, providing your
CNIC and issuance date is required.

Q: What if my CNIC is expired or I don’t have one?
A: A valid CNIC is mandatory to open a Halan account, as your information is verified
through NADRA.

Q: Where can I find my Halan account number?
A: Your Mobile number is your Halan account.

Q: Does my Halan account come with an IBAN?
A: Your Halan account automatically comes with an IBAN, which you can view in the app’s menu.

Q: What are the transaction and account limits?
A: Your daily transaction limit is set to PKR 100,000 by default.

To change your daily transaction limit, follow these steps:
From the home screen, open the Accounts tab, go to the Limits section, and tap on Manage Limits to adjust your limits.

Q: Are there any fees for using my Halan account?
A: The charges for different transactions can be found in the Schedule of Charges
section of the Halan app.

Q: What types of payments can I make through my account?
A: With your Halan account, you can make a variety of transactions including fund transfers,
bill payments, mobile top-ups (prepaid and postpaid), e-voucher purchases, and school or government fee payments.

Q: How can I view the transaction history of my account?
A: Go to the Accounts tab from the main menu, then open the Transaction History section to view your transaction history.

Q: Can I register more than one Halan account with the same CNIC or mobile number?
A: Only one Halan account can be opened with a single CNIC or mobile number.

Q: How can I withdraw money from my Halan account?
A: You can withdraw money by using below channel:
Using your Halan debit card at any 1Link ATM network across Pakistan
Biometric verification is mandatory for Debit Card issuance & cash deposit/withdrawal

Q: Can I pay utility bills through my account?
A: You can pay Electricity, Gas, Internet, Water and Telephone bills.

Q: Are there charges for cash withdrawals?
A: Charges are applicable when using ATMs on the 1LINK network. You can view the applicable charges in the Schedule of Charges section (SOC link).

Q: How do I add funds to my Halan account?
A: Money can be deposited free of cost using any of the below channels.
If you already have a bank account, you can transfer funds into your Halan account by using online banking or ATM.
Simply choose Halan Bank and add your Halan account number.
Deposit Cash by visiting any of our Halan bank Branch.
Funds will be deposited in your account instantly and you will get a confirmation SMS from 8457.

Q: What can I do with my Halan debit card?
A: Once you activate your Halan debit card, you can use it at ATMs and POS terminals nationwide.

Q: How can I apply for a Halan debit card?
A: You can order a Halan debit card through the Halan App.
Go to the Card tab in the main menu, tap on Add New Card, enter your name and delivery address, and submit your request.

Q: After placing an order, when will I receive my card?

A: It takes up to 7 working days for your debit card to arrive.
In case there’s a delay please call 021-111-111-776.

 

Q: How do I activate my debit card?

A: Once you receive your debit card, you can activate it through the Halan App.
Go to the Card tab in the main menu. Your card will be visible there—drag it down,
and you will be prompted to create a PIN. After setting the PIN, your card will be activated.

 

 

Q: What should I do if I need to block my card?

A: You can temporarily block your debit card from the Card Management section in the Halan App. For permanent blocking, please call 021-111-111-776.

Q: If my card is returned by the courier, what should I do?

A: If your card is returned, please call 021-111-111-776 and provide your details to have it re-dispatched. If the card is returned a second time, you will need to order a new debit card through the app.
Please note that the fee for the first card will not be refunded.

Q: I’m unable to log in to the Halan App. What should I do?

A: To log in to the Halan App, please ensure you have an active internet connection. If it doesn’t work on Wi-Fi, try switching to mobile data.
If the issue still persists, please contact us at 021-111-111-776.

Q: My Halan App is not working. What should I do?

A: It seems you might be using an older version of the Halan App. To fix this:
Open your app store.
Search for the Halan App and update it.
If the issue still persists, please contact our customer support center for further assistance.

Q: Is Touch ID available for logging into the Halan App?

A: Yes, if your phone supports it, you can enable Touch ID to log in to the Halan App once you’ve registered. You will still have the option to switch back to logging in with your Login PIN at any time.

Q: What are the rules for setting up a Login PIN?

A: Your login PIN must consist of 6 digits and cannot be the same as your previous PIN when changing it.

Q: How can I change my TPIN?

A: You can change your TPIN by opening the hamburger menu (H logo on the left side of the app).
Go to the Security tab, then select Change/Forgot TPIN.

Q: What if I forget my login PIN?
A: If you forget your login PIN, follow these steps:

  • On the login screen, tap Forgot Login PIN.
  • Enter your CNIC number.
  • Enter your mobile number.
  • Provide your CNIC issuance date.
  • Generate a new login PIN.

Q: How can I change my CardPIN?
A: You can change your card PIN by going to the Card tab on the home screen, tapping Change PIN, entering your current PIN, and setting a new one.

Q: What is RAAST?
A: Raast is Pakistan’s instant payment system introduced by the State Bank of Pakistan. It allows users to send and receive money instantly, securely, and at low cost through digital channels.

Q: What is a RAAST ID?
A: Raast ID is a simple identifier (currently your mobile number) linked to your bank account.
Instead of sharing long account or IBAN numbers, you can provide your Raast ID to others and instantly receive funds in your linked account.

Q: How do I send money using RAAST?
A: To send money through Raast, open the Halan App and go to the Send Money section. Select the Raast option, then enter the receiver’s Raast ID (mobile number) or IBAN, confirm the details, and proceed with the payment. The funds will be transferred instantly.

Q: How do I link my Halan account as my RAAST ID in the app?
A: To link your Halan account as your Raast ID:

  • Ensure your Raast ID (mobile number) is not already linked with another bank.
  • Open the Halan App and go to Send Money.
  • Select Raast.
  • Tap on ID Management.
  • Choose Link Account.
  • Select your mobile number registered with Halan to link it as your Raast ID.

Q: Can I link more than one account to my RAAST ID?
A: Bank account at a time. If you wish to connect it with another account, you will first need to De-link it from the existing one.

Q: If I already have another bank linked to my RAAST ID, how can I switch to Halan?
A: You will have to call on the helpline of that bank and request for deleting/de-linking your RAAST ID or de-link from their app.
Once your account is deleted/De-linked from there, you can log on to Halan app and link your Halan account as RAAST ID from “ID Management” inside RAAST menu.

Q: Do both the sender and receiver need to be registered with RAAST for the transfer to work?
A: No, only the receiver needs to be registered on Raast for you to send them money using their mobile number.
