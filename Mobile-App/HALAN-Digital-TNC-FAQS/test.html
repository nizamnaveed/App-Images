Q: What is Payvay?
A: Payvay is a digital financial services provider operating under a pilot license issued by the State Bank of Pakistan (SBP). Our platform offers secure and user-friendly solutions for essential financial transactions, including digital wallets, fund transfers, bill payments, and more.

Q: Is Payvay regulated by the State Bank of Pakistan (SBP)?
A: Yes, Payvay is fully regulated under the Electronic Money Institution (EMI) framework established by the SBP. This ensures our operations adhere to strict regulatory standards for security and compliance.

Q: Who is eligible to open an account with Payvay?
A: Any Pakistani citizen aged 18 or above with a valid Computerized National Identity Card (CNIC) or Smart National Identity Card (SNIC) can open an account through the Payvay mobile application.

Q: What services does Payvay offer?
A: Our services include:

� Secure digital wallet for money storage

� Instant fund transfers via Inter-Bank Funds Transfer (IBFT)

� Wallet-to-wallet transfers

� Bill payments

� Comprehensive account management through our mobile app

Q: How can I add funds to my Payvay wallet?
A: You can add money by initiating an IBFT from any bank account to your Payvay wallet.

Q: How can I withdraw funds from my Payvay wallet?
A: Funds can be withdrawn via:

� IBFT to any bank account

� Payvay Debit Card

Q: How can I close my Payvay account?
A: To close your account, please contact our customer support team. Ensure that your wallet balance is zero before initiating the closure process.

Q: Can I transfer funds to other banks?
A: Yes, Payvay supports Inter-Bank Funds Transfer (IBFT) to any bank or mobile account within Pakistan.

Q: Can I transfer funds to another Payvay wallet?
A: Absolutely. Wallet-to-wallet transfers between Payvay users are quick and secure.

Q: What types of payments can I make with Payvay?
A: You can pay utility bills, mobile top-ups, and other supported services directly through the Payvay app.

Q: Are transactions secure?
A: Yes. Payvay employs end-to-end encryption and adheres to industry-standard security protocols to protect your data and funds.
