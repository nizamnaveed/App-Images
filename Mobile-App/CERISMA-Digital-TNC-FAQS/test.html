Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • What is Payvay?
    Payvay is a digital financial services provider operating under a pilot license issued by the State Bank of Pakistan (SBP). Our platform offers secure and user-friendly solutions for essential financial transactions, including digital wallets, fund transfers, bill payments, and more.

    •  

  • Is Payvay regulated by the State Bank of Pakistan (SBP)?
    Yes, Payvay is fully regulated under the Electronic Money Institution (EMI) framework established by the SBP. This ensures our operations adhere to strict regulatory standards for security and compliance.

    •  

  • Who is eligible to open an account with Payvay?
    Any Pakistani citizen aged 18 or above with a valid Computerized National Identity Card (CNIC) or Smart National Identity Card (SNIC) can open an account through the Payvay mobile application.

    •  

  • What services does Payvay offer?
    A: Our services include:
  • Secure digital wallet for money storage
  • Instant fund transfers via Inter-Bank Funds Transfer (IBFT)
  • Wallet-to-wallet transfers
  • Bill payments
  • How can I add funds to my Payvay wallet?
    You can add money by initiating an IBFT from any bank account to your Payvay wallet.

    •  

  • How can I withdraw funds from my Payvay wallet?
    Funds can be withdrawn via:
  • IBFT to any bank account

  • How can I close my Payvay account?
    To close your account, please contact our customer support team. Ensure that your wallet balance is zero before initiating the closure process.

    •  

  • Can I transfer funds to other banks?
    Yes, Payvay supports Inter-Bank Funds Transfer (IBFT) to any bank or mobile account as well as Payvay users within Pakistan.

    •  

     

  • What types of payments can I make with Payvay?
    You can pay utility bills, mobile top-ups, and other supported services directly through the Payvay app.

    •  

  • Are transactions secure?
    Yes. Payvay employs end-to-end encryption and adheres to industry-standard security protocols to protect your data and funds.

    •  

  • What are the fees associated with Payvay services?
    For detailed information on fees, please refer to the "Schedule of Charges" available within the app or on our official website.

    •  

  • Is my personal data safe with Payvay?
    Yes. We follow stringent data protection policies and comply with the SBP’s regulatory standards for EMI operations to ensure your personal information remains secure.

    •  

  • What should I do if I suspect unauthorized access or a security issue?
    Immediately contact our support team and report the issue to help us safeguard your account.

    •  

  • How can I reset my app password?
